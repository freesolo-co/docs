## Response Times

- **Standard inquiries:** Freesolo replies within one business day. You'll hear from someone who can actually address your question — not an auto-responder.
- **Enterprise customers:** Dedicated support channels with 24/7 engineering coverage. Critical production issues are escalated immediately.

<Tip>
  If you're an enterprise customer with a time-sensitive issue, use your dedicated support channel rather than the general email address to ensure the fastest response.
</Tip>

## What to Include in Your Support Request

To help us resolve your issue as quickly as possible, include the following in your message:

- **Your organization and team name** — So we can look up your account and configuration.
- **What you're building** — A brief description of the workflow or feature you're working on (e.g., "fine-tuning a classification model on customer support tickets").
- **The issue** — What you expected to happen, what actually happened, and any error messages or logs you've seen.
- **Steps to reproduce** — If applicable, a sequence of actions that reliably triggers the issue.
- **Urgency** — Whether this is blocking production, blocking development, or a general question.

The more context you provide upfront, the faster we can help.

## Security Incidents

<Warning>
  If you believe your Freesolo credentials, API keys, or workspace have been compromised, notify us **immediately** at [founders@freesolo.co](mailto:founders@freesolo.co). Include "SECURITY INCIDENT" in the subject line.
</Warning>

Per Freesolo's Terms of Service, customers are responsible for notifying Freesolo promptly if credentials are compromised. Acting quickly limits the blast radius of a potential incident. Upon receiving your report, the engineering team will work with you to rotate credentials, audit access logs, and secure your account.

If you discover a security vulnerability in Freesolo itself — rather than a compromise of your own account — please also report it to [founders@freesolo.co](mailto:founders@freesolo.co) with a description of the issue. Responsible disclosure is appreciated and handled with care.
