## Get up and running

Follow these steps to go from sign-up to your first evaluated AI feature.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [freesolo.co/sign-up](https://freesolo.co/sign-up) using Google SSO, GitHub SSO, or an email and password. Your account is the entry point to all your projects and team settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your workspace">
    Create an organization, name your first project, and invite your teammates. Permissions and roles can be configured from the team settings panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your AI feature">
    Give your feature a name, describe its constraints, and attach the workflow context it will operate in. Freesolo uses this to instrument the feature accurately from day one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first evaluation">
    Choose your eval definitions, select a model, and kick off your first evaluation run. Freesolo measures output quality, latency, reliability, and cost side by side.
  </Step>
</Steps>
