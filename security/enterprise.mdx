Freesolo is built to handle the demands of enterprise AI development — not just proof-of-concept workloads. Production AI pipelines require predictable uptime, sufficient throughput, compliant data residency, and a support team that can actually resolve technical issues. This page describes the enterprise capabilities Freesolo provides and how to engage us for a formal agreement.

## High Availability SLAs

Freesolo's infrastructure is architected for high availability. Enterprise customers receive contractual SLAs that define uptime commitments for the platform's core services, giving your team a reliable foundation for production deployments.

<Info>
  Specific SLA percentages and remediation terms are defined in your enterprise agreement. Contact us at [founders@freesolo.co](mailto:founders@freesolo.co) or [book a call](https://cal.com/freesolo/chat) to discuss your uptime requirements before signing.
</Info>

## High Rate Limits

Standard API rate limits are designed for development and testing. Enterprise customers receive elevated rate limits tuned for production-scale AI workloads, including:

- High-volume evaluation pipelines running thousands of examples
- Continuous training jobs with frequent API interactions
- Multi-user teams working concurrently on the same platform
- Real-time inference in customer-facing product features

If your workload has specific throughput requirements, let us know during onboarding and we'll configure limits that match your needs.

## US-Based Infrastructure

All Freesolo infrastructure — including compute, storage, and data processing — is located within the United States. This means:

- Customer data does not leave US jurisdiction during processing
- Your team can satisfy data residency requirements common in regulated industries
- Compliance documentation reflects a single, predictable infrastructure footprint

<Note>
  If your organization requires specific cloud regions, data center certifications, or non-US residency for certain data types, contact us to discuss what is possible under a custom enterprise agreement.
</Note>

## Data Processing Agreements and Enterprise Contracts

For teams with legal, procurement, or compliance requirements, Freesolo offers:

- **Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)** — Formalizes how Freesolo processes personal data on your behalf, supporting GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulatory frameworks.
- **Order Forms** — Custom commercial agreements that capture your specific volume, SLA, and support commitments.
- **Security Documentation** — ISO 27001 and SOC 2 reports, infrastructure documentation, and questionnaire responses available under NDA.

To request any of these documents or begin a procurement process, email [founders@freesolo.co](mailto:founders@freesolo.co).
