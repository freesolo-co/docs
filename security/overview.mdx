Freesolo is built for enterprise teams who need rigorous security controls, predictable uptime, and verifiable compliance. Every layer of the platform — from infrastructure to access controls — is designed to meet the standards your security and legal teams require. This page summarizes our certifications, infrastructure posture, and the practices we follow to protect your data.

## Certifications

Freesolo has completed independent third-party audits and holds the following certifications:

- **ISO 27001** — Our information security management system (ISMS) meets the requirements of ISO/IEC 27001, the internationally recognized standard for systematic management of sensitive company information.
- **SOC 2 Type I** — An independent auditor has assessed and confirmed that our security controls are suitably designed as of a specific point in time.
- **SOC 2 Type II** — An independent auditor has confirmed that our security controls operated effectively over an extended observation period, providing stronger assurance of ongoing compliance.

Audit reports are available to enterprise customers under NDA. Contact [founders@freesolo.co](mailto:founders@freesolo.co) to request a copy.

## Third-Party Security Audits

<Note>
  Freesolo undergoes regular third-party penetration tests and security reviews conducted by independent security firms. Findings are remediated on a tracked schedule and reviewed by our engineering leadership.
</Note>

Beyond certification audits, we commission dedicated security assessments to identify and address vulnerabilities before they can impact customers. These reviews cover application security, infrastructure configuration, and access controls.
