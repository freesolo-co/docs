Your data belongs to you. Freesolo is designed so that enterprise teams can build, evaluate, train, and deploy AI models without sacrificing control over the content that powers those workflows. This page explains exactly what Customer Content is, how we use it, what we never do with it, and how to exercise your data rights.

## What Is Customer Content?

"Customer Content" refers to everything you bring into Freesolo to power your AI workflows. This includes, but is not limited to:

- Prompts and prompt templates
- Example inputs and outputs
- Datasets and training data
- Labels and annotations
- Evaluation definitions, traces, and logs
- Files and screenshots
- Model outputs
- Workflow data

Anything you submit to Freesolo to build, evaluate, train, or deploy your models is your Customer Content.

<Note>
  If you are using Freesolo on behalf of an organization, your organization controls the data submitted. Individual user accounts operate under the data governance policies your organization establishes.
</Note>

## You Retain Ownership

Freesolo does not claim ownership of your Customer Content. You retain all rights to the content you submit. By using the platform, you grant Freesolo the limited rights necessary to process your content in order to provide the services you've requested — nothing more.

## How Freesolo Uses Your Data

Freesolo processes your Customer Content strictly to deliver the platform's capabilities:

- **Provide and operate Freesolo** — Running evaluations, training pipelines, and deployments you configure.
- **Build, evaluate, train, and deploy AI models at your direction** — Your data powers the workflows you define; Freesolo executes those workflows on your behalf.
- **Manage your account and billing** — Associating usage with your account for invoicing and access control purposes.
- **Improve platform reliability** — Diagnosing errors, monitoring system health, and improving infrastructure performance.
- **Protect against abuse** — Detecting and preventing misuse of the platform.

## What Freesolo Does Not Do

<Warning>
  Freesolo does **not** sell your Customer Content to third parties under any circumstances.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  Freesolo does **not** use one customer's private Customer Content to train or improve models for another customer. Your private data stays private.
</Warning>

The only exceptions to data use are situations you explicitly authorize: content you make public, content you instruct us to use in a specific cross-customer or aggregate context, or data that has been fully de-identified and aggregated such that it cannot be attributed to you.

## Data Collected Automatically

In addition to Customer Content you actively submit, Freesolo collects certain operational data automatically to run the platform:

- **Account details** — Name, email, organization, and authentication information.
- **Billing information** — Payment details processed through our billing provider.
- **Product configuration data** — Settings, integrations, and preferences you configure.
- **Usage events** — Feature interactions and workflow executions within the platform.
- **API request metadata** — Request timestamps, endpoint paths, response codes, and latency — but not the payload content of your API calls unless you deliberately submit it as part of a workflow.
- **Error logs** — Diagnostic information generated when the platform encounters errors.

## Data Retention and Deletion

You can request access to, export of, or deletion of your data at any time. To submit a data request, contact us at [founders@freesolo.co](mailto:founders@freesolo.co). We will respond to data requests in accordance with applicable law and our contractual commitments.

<Tip>
  Enterprise customers can negotiate specific data retention schedules and deletion SLAs as part of their contracts. Reach out to [founders@freesolo.co](mailto:founders@freesolo.co) to discuss your requirements.
</Tip>
